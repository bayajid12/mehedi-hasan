# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name**: SM Mehedi Hasan (@webmehedi) Portfolio Website
**Objective**: To build a premium, high-performance static personal portfolio website that highlights the 20+ years of expertise, business leadership, community contributions, and technical acumen of S.M. Mehedi Hasan.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Business Prospects & Clients**: Looking for IT solutions, custom software, and digital transformation via Coder71 Ltd.
- **Freelancers & Mentees**: Individuals from the Fiverr Bangladesh Community or Founders Hub seeking inspiration and mentorship.
- **Event Organizers & Media**: People looking to book Mehedi Hasan for talks, podcasts, or seminars.

## 3. Technology Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js (using App Router, configured for static HTML export)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS for utility-first styling
- **Typography**: 
  - Display & Titles: **Bebas Neue** (To give a bold, striking presence)
  - Body Text: **Inter** (For maximum readability and a clean modern look)
- **Deployment**: Static Site Generation (SSG)

## 4. Key Content & Persona Details
- **Identity**: Distinguished technology entrepreneur, strategic leader, community mentor.
- **Key Distinction**: Bridging technical innovation with community empowerment.
- **Business Leadership**: 
  - Managing Director at Coder71 Ltd. (Since 2015)
  - Co-Founder of Founders Hub
  - Former CEO & Chairperson of BD Web Solutions, former roles at Bashundhara Group, New York/UK firms.
- **Community & Public Service**:
  - 2025 President of JCI Dhaka Founders (Star Local President Award 2025).
  - Fiverr Bangladesh Community Leader (2022-2025): Mentored thousands, 5000+ orders personally completed.
  - BASIS Member.
- **Expertise**: End-to-end digital transformation, custom-built digital products (retail, e-commerce, manufacturing).
- **Philosophy**: "Your network is your net worth."

## 5. Website Architecture (Pages & Sections)
### 5.1 Home
- **Hero Section**: High-impact introduction, bold typography (Bebas Neue), professional photo, clear CTA.
- **Stats Section**: Highlighting key metrics (e.g., 20+ Yrs Exp, 5000+ Orders, etc.).
- **About Me Section**: Brief overview of his journey and philosophy.
- **Career Timeline**: Visual representation of his professional journey.
- **Career Journey**: Deeper dive into his roles.
- **Behind the Scene Gallery**: A glimpse into his life, workflow, and networking.
- **Media and Talks**: Videos, podcasts, and speaking engagements.
- **Footer with CTA**: Direct links to LinkedIn, Facebook, Coder71 Ltd., and quick links.

### 5.2 About Me
- Comprehensive biography, elaborated business leadership, and community service details.

### 5.3 Blogs
- Listing of articles, insights, and thoughts on technology, freelancing, and leadership.
- **Blog Details**: Individual dynamic pages for blog reading.

### 5.4 Gallery
- A visual portfolio of events, JCI activities, Fiverr community meetups, and personal milestones.

### 5.5 Contact
- Form and direct contact information for business inquiries, speaking engagements, and networking.

## 6. Design Aesthetics
- **Vibe**: Professional, authoritative, modern, clean, and inspiring.
- **Responsiveness**: Mobile-first approach, fully responsive across all screen sizes.
- **Animations**: Subtle, smooth micro-interactions to make the static site feel dynamic without being overwhelming.
